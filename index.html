Ceremony info and directions

Gifts gift giving and registry

On our wedding day we celebrate the overflowing of our cup of blessings. On this day of abundance and thanksgiving, we invite you to consider celebrating our commitment to each other and our commitment to the world by giving generously to one of the following organizations. These are organizations that are significant to us as a couple. Giving to them would truly be the most meaningful way to honor our wedding day.

Haitian Timoun Foundation (HTF). The work of this organization supports children in Haiti, including the children who find their home in the St. Joseph Family - former street children, child slaves, and individuals with disabilities. In the summer of 2010, both of us had the opportunity to visit two of the St. Joseph family homes in Port-au-Prince and we continue to be blessed by the relationships that began on that trip. Haiti will always be close to our hearts.

The Lutheran Center (NeLCM-Lincoln). A long, long time ago, Josh's parents found the Lutheran Center to be a church home while at college. They met there and fell in love. Now throughout our college years, The Lutheran Center has been a place to foster and strengthen our own relationship. We go to ask questions, to think deeply about God and the world, and to consider our vocations as disciples. The conversations and relationships that have developed in this place have been incredibly formational for our faith and for our love.

Clinic With A Heart is a free medical clinic serving the medically uninsured and underinsured populations of Lincoln. Erin currently volunteers at the clinic as Spanish-English translator and is passionate about the importance of the work that the clinic does.

We have also registered for gifts at Bed, Bath, and Beyond and Target.
